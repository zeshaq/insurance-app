Claim filing — slices 9 + 10

Filing a claim moves bytes through almost every platform service in one request: the photo streams into MinIO; Liberty calls MI's OCR mediator (which talks to a WireMock vision stub) for extracted text; if you give an otherPartyVin, Liberty also does an mTLS handshake against partner-mock and gets cross-carrier coverage data back. The claim row commits to Postgres; Debezium CDC pushes it to OpenSearch a few seconds later (slice 14, try the OpenSearch Dashboards).
