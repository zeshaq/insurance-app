Where the flows live

Where to click for a full end-to-end exercise. Two valid starting points; pick whichever is more natural for what you're teaching.

  • From the customer side — sign in at the customer portal, get a quote, bind it, pay against the policy, then file a claim with a photo. Each action surfaces in Kafka UI / Mailpit / MinIO / OpenSearch immediately.
  • From the agent side — sign in at the agent dashboard, flip the Claims filter to FILED, click Approve. The audit snapshot under audit-events (Kafka UI) flips to APPROVED in real time.
  • From the teaching tour — the overview page has clickable cards for the lower-level API operations (POST /api/quotes, @Retry/DLQ payments, multipart claim upload, etc.) with no login required.
