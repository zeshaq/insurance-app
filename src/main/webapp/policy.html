Policy bind — slice 6

Binding a Quote into a Policy is the slice that introduces two new enterprise patterns: a Redlock distributed lock (keyed by quoteId) prevents concurrent binds, and the resulting record lands on a log-compacted Kafka topic (policy-events, keyed by policyNumber) so compaction collapses multiple updates of the same policy to one record.
