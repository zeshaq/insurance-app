Reporting — slice 12

Two paired enterprise patterns. A Kafka Streams topology inside Liberty subscribes to payment-events, groups by status, and counts in 1-minute tumbling windows into a materialized in-memory state store. A scheduled MI task (synapse Quartz, every 30 s) POSTs to /api/reports/snapshot which reads the state store totals and persists a report_run row.
