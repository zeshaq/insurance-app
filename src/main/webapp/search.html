Search — slice 14

Every claim row in Postgres rides a CDC pipeline to OpenSearch: Debezium reads logical replication on the claim table, publishes envelopes to the Kafka topic dbserver1.public.claim, and SearchIndexer (a Liberty bean) writes them into the OpenSearch claims index. Queries here go through /api/search/claims?q=… which does a multi_match across description, ocr_text, policy_number, and the two VIN columns.

What to watch

Open OpenSearch Dashboards → Discover → index claims to see the same data OpenSearch-side. File a new claim from the claim page, wait ~5 seconds, search for a unique word from its description here — the CDC pipeline catches up quickly.
